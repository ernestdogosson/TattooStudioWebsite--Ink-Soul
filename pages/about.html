About Us

Shop Story

Ink & Soul was founded in 2018 in the heart of Helsingborg, Sweden. What started as a small studio with a single chair has grown into one of the city's most respected tattoo parlors. Our passion for art and dedication to the craft drives everything we do. We believe every tattoo tells a story, and we take pride in helping our clients bring their visions to life. Whether it's your first piece or your fiftieth, we treat every session with the same care and attention to detail.

Our Artists

Grumpy

Specializing in black and grey realism with over 10 years of experience. Known for bringing portraits and nature scenes to life on skin.

Baby Face

A master of fine line and minimalist work. Baby Face brings a delicate touch and an eye for clean, elegant designs.

Rarrr

Bold colors and neo-traditional style are Rarrr's signature. With a background in illustration, every piece is packed with energy and detail.

Values & Mission

We believe in creating a welcoming space where self-expression thrives. Our mission is to deliver exceptional tattoo art while building lasting relationships with our clients.

Hygiene & Safety

Your safety is our top priority. We use single-use needles, hospital-grade sterilization, and follow all health regulations to ensure a clean, safe experience every time.
